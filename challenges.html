What is a Foundation Challenge

  • To encourage participation in the NHS Foundation and plan for the Spring Gala, the Foundation “challenges” parents and community members to support NHS financially. If you accept a “Challenge,” you agree to be responsible for raising $1,000 through a personal a tax-deductible donation to the Foundation, or soliciting outside donations and Gala ticket sales totaling $1,000.
  • Your challenge donation will entitle you to ten (10) tickets based on your donation level.
  • If you sell any Gala tickets to parents and community members, please have them make their checks payable to NHS Foundation for Excellence. All of those checks to the Foundation should be turned in to our treasurer before the Gala.

Being a Challenger has Its Benefits

YOU RECEIVE:

  • Gala Tickers (up to 10)
  • Recognition on Web Site
  • Recognition at Gala
  • Evening of Appreciation Event
