Wish Bricks

The art students of Norcross High School have painted a "SCHOOL HOUSE" on canvas and teachers submit requests for items they in their classrooms to enhance the learning experience for their students. The requests are written on "BRICKS" that are placed on the school house. During the annual Gala, members of the community purchase these "bricks" and the Foundation ensures that those funds are directed to that teacher or department for their specific need. This past spring, supporters purchased over "$28,000 IN BRICKS!"
